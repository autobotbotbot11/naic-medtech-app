UI Explorations

Pick the feeling before we touch the real app.

These are standalone HTML samples only. Nothing here changes the live app. Each sample uses the same `Records Home` content so you can judge the shell, hierarchy, buttons, cards, and overall product feel without getting distracted by feature differences.

Direction 1

Quiet Premium Clinic

Warm premium surfaces, restrained luxury, and stronger brand presence without becoming flashy.

  • Best fit if you want expensive-looking calmness.
  • Strongest path for future branded print alignment.
  • Feels boutique and trustworthy rather than corporate.
View sample

Direction 2

Modern Clinical Workspace

Sharper, cooler, and more workstation-like. Clean, efficient, and safest for dense operational screens.

  • Strongest for clarity and operational efficiency.
  • Easiest to scale across more screens later.
  • Lower risk, but slightly less memorable.
View sample

Direction 3

Warm Editorial Healthcare

More authored and design-forward, with a soft paper-like mood and stronger visual personality.

  • Most memorable and custom-feeling.
  • Great if you want more visual identity in the shell.
  • Needs discipline so it does not overpower dense forms.
View sample

Direction 4

Modern Clinical Depth

The same modern clinical base, but with a darker shell, deeper contrast, and more premium atmosphere without becoming full dark mode.

  • Good if the clean modern direction feels too flat.
  • Adds atmosphere without the maintenance burden of theme switching.
  • Likely the best bridge between modern and premium.
View sample

Direction 5

Clinical Depth Luxe

The richer, more luxurious version of the depth direction. More premium shell drama, softer cards, stronger wow factor.

  • Best if you want the most premium client-facing first impression.
  • Closer to a boutique custom product than standard med software.
  • Slightly riskier if you want very utilitarian density later.
View sample

Direction 5B

Clinical Depth Luxe Dark

The dark counterpart of the luxe direction: same premium identity, but tuned for eye comfort and longer work sessions.

  • Best if the luxe direction is right, but white-heavy screens still feel tiring.
  • Should feel like the same product, not a separate theme experiment.
  • Good fit for long medtech sessions if the contrast stays soft and controlled.
View sample

Direction 5C

Clinical Depth Luxe Darker

A deeper, moodier version of the dark luxe direction. More dramatic shell, lower luminance, and stronger night-work atmosphere.

  • Best if you want the dark mode to feel more cinematic and premium.
  • Better for mood, but must still be checked against long-session readability.
  • Good comparison point before we lock the final dark theme.
View sample

Direction 6

Clinical Depth Focus

The more operational version of the depth direction. Keeps the dark shell and premium feel, but tighter and more medtech-workstation focused.

  • Best if you want daily usability to stay the priority.
  • Feels premium, but a bit more disciplined and work-focused.
  • Likely easiest to scale across forms and settings later.
View sample
